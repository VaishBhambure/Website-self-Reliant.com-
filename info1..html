IIT (Indian Institute of Technology) Bombay

IIT (Indian Institute of Technology) Bombay is one of the oldest engineering institutes in India. IIT Bombay was established in 1958, and it is the premier institute in India to be established with foreign assistance. In 1961, IIT Bombay was declared as the Institute of National Importance by the Indian government. After attaining the premier status, IIT Bombay has shown excellence in academics, research and overall development of students. Among IITs, IIT Bombay has an impressive record and achievements in research, academics, and international outlook. IIT Bombay has been successful in emerging as a leader in the field of engineering education and research. The performance of IIT Bombay in global rankings over the years is one of the major reasons for an increase in the number of international students. At the same time, the performance of IIT Bombay in NIRF rankings is also noteworthy.
